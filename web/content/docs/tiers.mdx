## How licensing works

The license file lives at `~/.pushkey/.license` (AES-GCM encrypted). The desktop app refreshes the token every 24h via a heartbeat call.

**Offline grace period:** 10 days. After 10 days without a heartbeat, the tier downgrades to Free until the next successful refresh.

## Check your tier

```bash
pushkey license
```
